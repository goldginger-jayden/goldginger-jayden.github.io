What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotion Intelligence relates to the ability of understanding and managing your own emotions, and through this, being able to understand and affect the emotions of others.

How is it Different to IQ?

IQ relates to general intelligence, analytical thinking, logic and reasoning etc...

Why is Emotional Intelligence Important?

Having a good level of emotional intelligence is important as it allows you to better perceive and regulate your own emotional state, and in turn, read other people better.
